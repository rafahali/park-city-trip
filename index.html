July 4th Fireworks

Sat Park City Mountain Resort After dark

Park City Mountain Resort runs the main fireworks show at 10pm on July 4th. Visible from the slopes and from much of town. The Montage terrace has a strong sightline. Go early if you want a Main Street position—it gets dense by 9pm. Walking back uphill after is steep; consider the shuttle.

Book via Montage concierge

Guided trail ride through Deer Valley wildflowers at ~8,200 ft. Best in July when the alpine meadows are blooming. Book through the Montage concierge—limited spots. Goes early (7:30 or 9am), which works as the Saturday morning before the pool afternoon.

Morning Hike from Montage

Fri or Sat morning ~1.5 hrs

The Montage has direct trailhead access. The Jordanelle Overlook trail is 3 miles with views of the reservoir and the Uintas. Go before 9am before it gets warm. Altitude hits—go slower than you think you need to and drink water before you leave.

Deer Valley Gondola

Fri or Sat Mountain views

Deer Valley runs the gondola in summer for sightseeing and mountain biking. The top at ~9,400 ft has a panoramic view over Park City and the Wasatch range. 20 minutes from Montage, quick turnaround.

05

Tips

Altitude: Park City sits at 7,000–8,200 ft. First day effects are real even if you’re fit—headache, fatigue, shortness of breath on stairs. Drink twice the water you think you need. Avoid heavy drinking the first evening. By Saturday you’re acclimated.

July 4th weekend crowds: Park City’s busiest weekend of the year. Main Street fills by 7pm on Saturday. If you’re going to the fireworks, take the Montage shuttle and arrive by 9pm for a good position. Don’t plan to drive on Main Street after 8pm.

Weather: July days are warm (75–85°F), evenings cool quickly to 50s at elevation. Bring a light jacket for any outdoor dinner or the fireworks. Afternoon thunderstorms are common—anything outdoor in the afternoon has a 30–40% chance of a brief storm. They pass fast.

Sunday departures: Mallika leaves SLC at 7:50am—she needs to be in the car by 6:15am at the latest. Rafah (10:25am) and Anya (11:13am) can share a late-morning Lyft. Checkout is 11am; ask the Montage for a late checkout or leave bags with bell staff and use the lobby until your Lyft arrives.

Main Street parking: impossible July 4th weekend. Never drive to Main Street—always take the Montage shuttle or a Lyft. The shuttle returns last pickup around midnight; confirm timing with the concierge on arrival.

Spa gratuity: 18–20% is standard on the service total, added to the room bill. Cash tip directly to your therapist is appreciated on top if the session was exceptional.

No alcohol at grocery stores in Utah: wine and spirits only at state liquor stores (DABC). The nearest is in Kimball Junction (~10 min). Hours are 11am–10pm Mon–Sat, closed Sunday. Stock up Thursday if you want anything for the room. The Montage bar is fully stocked but expensive.
